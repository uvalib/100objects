The 101st Object

Is “100 Objects” enough? Absolutely not. From the exhibition’s earliest planning phase, we’ve heard cries of protest that something essential is missing from the list.

If you could add an item to this exhibition, what would it be? A famous monument on Grounds? A document in the archives describing an incident of great significance? Something you have kept from your own time at UVA? Share it here or tweet it to @rareUVA #100objects.
