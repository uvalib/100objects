Get the App

Download the University of Virginia Walking Tours of Grounds app to discover all 100 Objects at over a dozen sites around grounds through the 100 Objects Walking Tour. The Walking Tour, published through MyTours, will lead you through the University’s historic grounds, from the exhibition’s base in the Harrison Institute and Small Special Collections Library to the Rotunda, sites in the Academical Village, memorials and statues around Grounds, and exhibitions in Alumni Hall, the Darden School of Business, and the UVA School of Law.

For questions about the Walking Tours of Grounds app, please contact Holly Robertson, Exhibitions Coordinator.
